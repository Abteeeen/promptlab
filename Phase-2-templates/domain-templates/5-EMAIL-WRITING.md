# 📱 EMAIL WRITING PROMPT TEMPLATE

**Best For**: Campaign emails, newsletters, promotional content

## 🎯 TEMPLATE

```
You are an expert email copywriter specializing in [INDUSTRY] conversions.
Your specialty: High-open-rate, high-click-rate email that feels personal.

EMAIL PURPOSE: [WELCOME / PROMOTION / EDUCATIONAL / NURTURE / RE-ENGAGEMENT]

RECIPIENT:
- Segment: [New subscribers / Customers / Inactive / VIP]
- Industry: [IF APPLICABLE]
- Pain point: [MAIN ISSUE]
- Desired outcome: [WHAT SHOULD THEY DO]

EMAIL OBJECTIVE:
- Primary goal: [SPECIFIC ACTION: sign up / buy / download / open next email]
- Success metric: [OPEN RATE % / CLICK RATE % / CONVERSION %]

CONTENT REQUIREMENTS:
- Subject line: [OPTIONAL: direction on tone]
- Preview text: [OPTIONAL: what should show in preview]
- From name: [PERSON NAME, not company]
- Length: [WORD COUNT or SHORT/MEDIUM/LONG]
- Include CTA count: [1 - keep focus]
- Tone: [PROFESSIONAL / FRIENDLY / URGENT / EDUCATIONAL]
- Format: [TEXT / HTML / MINIMAL FORMATTING]

EMAIL STRUCTURE:
1. Subject line: [Hook + curiosity / Value + urgency / Question that resonates]
2. Greeting: [Personalize if possible]
3. Introduction (1 paragraph): [Hook = problem they face]
4. Body (2-3 paragraphs): [Solution approach / How it works / Benefits]
5. CTA section (2-3 lines): [Clear action + benefit of action]
6. Closing: [Professional sign-off + signature]
7. Footer: [Unsubscribe required by law - don't hide it]

CTA DETAILS:
- Button text: [SPECIFIC ACTION: "Start free trial" not "Learn more"]
- Button color: [OPTIONAL]
- Link destination: [CLEAR, no surprise redirects]
- Urgency element: [LIMITED TIME / ONLY FOR YOU / ACT TODAY or NONE]

A/B TEST THIS:
- Subject line variation: [ALTERNATIVE SUBJECT]
- CTA text variation: [ALTERNATIVE CTA]

TONE EXAMPLES:
- Show 1-2 sentences in desired voice
- Help model match your brand

COMPLIANCE:
- Include: Unsubscribe link (legal requirement)
- Avoid: [FORBIDDEN WORDS if any: e.g., URGENT in ALL CAPS]
- Verify: No spam trigger words excessively
```

## 🎨 REAL EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Welcome Email Series
```
You are a SaaS onboarding expert.

EMAIL 1 of 5: Welcome Email (sent immediately after signup)

RECIPIENT: New signup to productivity app
- They just signed up
- Haven't used app yet
- Goal: Get them to complete first task (builds habit)

OBJECTIVE:
- Primary: Get them to log in and complete first task
- Secondary: Reduce churn (first 7 days critical)
- Metric: 50%+ open rate, 15%+ click rate, 30%+ complete first task

CONTENT:
- Subject line: [Make it warm, personal, not hype-y]
  - "Welcome! Here's your first task (takes 2 min)" (good)
  - "AMAZING NEWS!!! WELCOME!!!!" (bad - spammy)
- From: [Founder name] or [Product manager name] - feels personal
- Length: 100-150 words (short, easy to digest)
- Tone: Friendly, encouraging, gives quick win

CONTENT SHOULD:
- Acknowledge: "You took the leap - thanks for trying us"
- Quick win: "Here's the fastest way to see value (2 minutes)"
- Social proof: "1,000+ people just completed this today"
- CTA: "Complete your first task now →"

EMAIL 4 of 5: Feature Email (Day 3-4)

OBJECTIVE:
- Show second feature that drives retention
- Goal: 25%+ try this feature

CONTENT:
- Show one feature in detail
- Why it saves time
- Social proof: "Most popular feature"
- CTA: "Try this feature"

EMAIL 5 of 5: "Help if you get stuck" (Day 6)

OBJECTIVE:
- Safety net before they churn
- Goal: Open conversation, not hard sell

CONTENT:
- "Getting stuck? Here's help"
- Resources + FAQ
- Calendar link to chat (if premium support)
- Tone: Helpful, not pushy
```

### Example 2: Promotional Email
```
You are a conversion-focused email marketer.

EMAIL: Black Friday Flash Sale

RECIPIENT: Existing customers + email list
- Love your product
- Some lapsed (haven't purchased in 3+ months)
- Goal: $X revenue this weekend

OBJECTIVE:
- Convert sales: $[X] revenue
- Metric: 5%+ conversion rate
- Open rate target: 30%+
- Click rate target: 8%+

SUBJECT LINE:
- Should hint at scarcity (Limited time / This weekend only)
- Should hint at value (50% off / Biggest sale)
- Should NOT be: ALL CAPS, URGENT!!!, Too salesy
- Sweet spot: "50% off this weekend (starts tomorrow)"

CONTENT:
- Headline: [Benefit + urgency]
- Proof: Show [% off], [How many units on sale], [Day/time sale ends]
- Social proof: "Selling fast - 500+ purchased"
- Product highlights: [3-4 best products + discount]
- CTA: "Shop now - ends Sunday midnight"
- Footer: "This is a one-time offer to you"

A/B VERSIONS:
- Subject A: "Black Friday: 50% off starts tomorrow"
- Subject B: "Save big - 50% off all weekend"

- CTA A: "Shop now"
- CTA B: "Grab your discount (ends Sunday)"

SEND TIMES:
- Friday 9am (Friday morning)
- Saturday 10am (weekend shoppers)
- Sunday 4pm (last chance)
```

### Example 3: Educational Newsletter
```
You are a newsletter writer for [INDUSTRY].

EMAIL: Weekly tips newsletter

RECIPIENT: Subscribers interested in [TOPIC]
- Open this regularly (they chose to subscribe)
- Goal: Keep them engaged + drive conversions over time

OBJECTIVE:
- Open rate: 30%+ (educational content)

- Keep engaged: Low unsubscribe (< 0.5%)

SUBJECT LINES:

CONTENT STRUCTURE:

- Hook: "This week we're covering..."

- Tip 2: [Actionable tip + example] (200 words)
- Tip 3: [Actionable tip + example] (200 words)
- Wrap-up: "Try these this week"
- Soft CTA: "Learn more in our [RESOURCE]" (not hard sell)

TONE: Helpful teacher, not salesperson
```

## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Subject Lines Are Everything**
   - 30-50% of opens/clicks is subject line
   - Test 2 versions always
   - Curiosity + Promise = best approach

2. **Keep It Short**
   - 100-150 words ideal
   - Scannable paragraphs
   - One clear CTA

3. **Personalize Beyond Name**
   - Show you know them: "You started [X]"
   - Acknowledge their business: "Your industry is [X]"
   - Reference past behavior: "Last month you [X]"

4. **Make CTA Obvious**
   - Pretty buttons work better than links
   - CTA should "pop" visually
   - Only have ONE primary CTA

5. **Test, Don't Guess**
   -Test subject lines
   - Test send times
   - Test CTA text
   - Let data guide you

6. **Mobile First**
   - 60%+ opens on mobile
   - Subject fits first 50 chars
   - CTA button large enough to tap
   - Images load fast

## 📊 EFFECTIVENESS METRICS

**Open Rate**: 25-35% (good), 15-25% (okay), <15% (needs work)  
**Click Rate**: 3-5% (good), 1-3% (okay), <1% (needs work)  
**Conversion**: 1-5% of clicks convert (depends on offer)  
**Unsubscribe**: <0.5% is good, >1% is problem
