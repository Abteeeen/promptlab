## 🎯 THE OPPORTUNITY

### Market Problem
Prompt engineering is **trial-and-error**, not systematic:
- 40% success rate with poor prompts
- 90% success rate with good prompts
- **50% performance variance** based on prompt quality alone
- No standard practices, no teaching methodology
- **Huge pain point**: Users struggle with "how do I ask the LLM correctly?"

### Our Solution
**Free, open-source web platform** that makes prompt engineering scientific:
1. **10 domain-specific templates** (not generic)
2. **Real-time quality scoring** (30-point system)
3. **Custom prompt generator** (intelligent matching)
4. **Analytics tracking** (know what works)
5. **Scientific foundation** (45 principles backed by research)

### Market Differentiation
**vs competitors like originality.ai**:
- ✅ 10 specialized domains (they: generic)
- ✅ Real-time 30-point scoring (they: no feedback)
- ✅ Scientific backing (they: marketing hype)
- ✅ Free unlimited tier (they: limited free)
- ✅ Advanced features (they: basic generation)
